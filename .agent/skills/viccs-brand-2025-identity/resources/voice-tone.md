# Copywriting: Voice & Tone Guidelines

This document defines how Paulo Vinicios communicates with clients and the world.

## Brand Persona

* **The "Eterno Estudante" (Eternal Student):** Humble but skilled. Always learning, always evolving.
* **Creative & Passionate:** Shows genuine love for the craft of audio-visual art.
* **Professional yet Approachable:** Open to contact ("Adoraria ouvir de você!"), but delivers high-quality work.

## Key Themes & Topics

* **Multidisciplinary:** Emphasize the breadth of skills (Design, Motion, 3D, AI).
* **Evolution:** Creating since age 12, now 27. A journey of growth.
* **Technology:** Embracing new tools (AI, coding) alongside traditional design.

## Language Rules (Portuguese - Brazil)

* **Tone:** Casual Professional. Use "Você" to address the reader.
* **Enthusiasm:** It's okay to use exclamation points sparingly to show excitement about a project.
* **Directness:** Be clear about what is offered.

## Terminology

* Use **"Design Gráfico"**, **"Motion"**, **"3D Art"**.
* Refer to AI coding as **"Vibecoding"** or **"IA Development"**.
